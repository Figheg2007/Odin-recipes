Green Bean Casserole

Description

Every Thanksgiving should have a green bean casserole, and this simple recipe makes one of the best you will ever eat. Don't beleive that it will be simple well how about If i say four ingrediants. Sounds simple enough now dosen't it. But seriosly the main dish I look forward to every year is this green bean casserole.

Ingrediants

  • Green beans: Two cans of drained green beans.
  • Canned soup: One can of condensed cream of mushroom soup.
  • Cheese: Sharp shredded cheddar.
  • French-fried onions: The fried onions provide the crunch.

Steps

  1. Mix the beans and soup in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave until warm.
  2. Stir in half the cheese. Microwave until melted and well-blended.
  3. Transfer to a prepared baking dish. Top with fried onions and remaing cheese.
  4. Bake in oven preheated to 350 degrees F until cheese is melted and onions are brown.
  5. Enjoy
