Corn Beef Hash

Description

In my personal opinion corn beef hash is one of the best breakfastes. It always tastes the same which is good, even when it comes from a can. But today we will be making a homeade version of one of the best dish every made. Luckly it is quite simple to make and only requires several ingrediants.

Ingrediants

  • Unsalted butter: Three tablespoons
  • Diced sweet onion: One cup
  • Minced garlic clove: One clove
  • Diced cooked corn beef: Two cups
  • Cooked yukon gold potaoes: Two cups
  • Horseradish: One tablespoon
  • Chopped fresh parsley: Two tablespoons
  • Black pepper: One eighth teaspoon

Steps

  1. In a large cast iron skillet, melt butter over medium-high heat. Add onion and sauté until softened and translucent, about 3 minutes. Stir in the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 20 seconds.
  2. Stir in the cooked potatoes and corned beef; gently mix together, spread out evenly in the skillet, then press down with a spatula.
  3. Let everything cook without stirring, allowing to brown, for about 3 minutes (use the spatula to take a quick peek underneath to make sure they’re browning, but also not burning.) Once browned, flip sections over to brown on the other side, pressing down again; cook for another 3 minutes.
  4. Add in the horseradish, parsley, and pepper and gently stir until combined.
  5. Enjoy
